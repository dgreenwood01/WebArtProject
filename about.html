Modus Operandi

Here at I/C U we are dedicated to helping our customers save money when it comes to heating and cooling their homes. Our employees are devoted to tracking you day and night so they know where you are in your house. Based on your location at all times, we make sure the room you are in at your house is your chosen temperature. No need to ever touch the thermostat so sit back and relax while we follow your every move!

How We Do It

Our system of tracking where our customers are is pretty simple. When a customer signs up to use I/C U, you download the app and you must allow the app to access your location at all times. This way our servers can follow you in your footsteps at all times. Our job is then simple, follow you around and change the thermostat to your desired temperatures and allow you the comfort you deserve in your home.
